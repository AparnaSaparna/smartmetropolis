      Christ Nagar Higher Secondary school started in 1976 as a private English Medium School in Trivandrum, India, recognised by the state government of Kerala as a minority educational institution. In 2002, Christ Nagar High School was upgraded to higher secondary level.     • Trivandrum International School

      Trivandrum International School, commonly known as TRINS, is a prestigious educational institution located on the outskirts of Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. Established with a vision of providing world-class education, the school follows an international curriculum, blending academic rigor with a focus on creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness. TRINS offers state-of-the-art facilities, a diverse student community, and a wide range of extracurricular activities, ensuring a well-rounded education.

      Holy Angels' School in Thiruvananthapuram is a renowned educational institution with a rich history of academic excellence and character development.     Training Skill and Development Centers

    CADDesk Trivandrum

    CADDesk in Trivandrum is a leading training center specializing in computer-aided design (CAD) and related software courses. Known for its industry-relevant curriculum and experienced instructors, CADDesk offers training programs in various CAD software, including AutoCAD, Revit, and SolidWorks, catering to students, engineers, and professionals. The institute provides hands-on learning experiences, ensuring that participants gain practical skills and knowledge that are directly applicable to real-world projects. With its focus on quality education and skill development, CADDesk has become a preferred choice for those looking to enhance their expertise in CAD and design technologies in Trivandrum.
